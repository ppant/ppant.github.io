  * [Rakudo on JVM](http://jnthn.net/papers/2013-yapceu-jvm.pdf)
  * [Concurrency, Parallelism and Asynchrony](http://jnthn.net/papers/2013-yapceu-conc.pdf)
  * [MoarVM](http://jnthn.net/papers/2013-yapceu-moarvm.pdf)
